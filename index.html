Subterranean Termites

Structural Risk: Extreme

Termites operate silently inside wooden foundations. By the time visual signs appear, thousands of dollars in drywall and framing damage may already be done.

Key Indicators

  • Mud tubes along concrete crawlspaces
  • Hollow-sounding structural timbers
  • Discarded translucent wings near window sills

Pro Prevention Tips

  • Redirect gutter downspouts away from home perimeter
  • Maintain a 6-inch gap between soil and wooden siding
  • Schedule annual inspections of crawlspaces and basements
